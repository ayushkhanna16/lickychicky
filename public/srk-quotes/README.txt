SRK QUOTE IMAGES
================

Store 6 romantic SRK quote images here (one for each month).

File naming:
- month1.jpg (for Month 1 - August 2025)
- month2.jpg (for Month 2 - September 2025)
- month3.jpg (for Month 3 - October 2025)
- month4.jpg (for Month 4 - November 2025)
- month5.jpg (for Month 5 - December 2025)
- month6.jpg (for Month 6 - January 2026)

Image requirements:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Recommended size: 800x600px or larger (landscape orientation works best)
- The quote text will be overlaid on the image, so choose images that work well with text overlay
- Romantic/beautiful images that match the SRK quote theme

The quotes are already configured in src/data/srkQuotes.js:
- Month 1: "Pyaar dosti hai..." (Kuch Kuch Hota Hai)
- Month 2: "Itni shiddat se..." (Om Shanti Om)
- Month 3: "Bade Bade deshon mein..." (DDLJ)
- Month 4: "Naina kaash mein..." (Kal Ho Na Ho)
- Month 5: "Ja Simran ja..." (DDLJ)
- Month 6: "Tum meri ho..." (Kal Ho Na Ho)

After adding the images, they will automatically appear when each room is unlocked!
